About us

In Central Asia, we are the industry leader in the production of carpets and rugs.

SAG (SAM ANTEP GILAM, OOO) is a full-cycle enterprise for the production of carpets and carpet products in Central Asia. All processes, from the beginning of the production process, to the packaging of the final carpet products, are cooled at the enterprise. The carpet is made of modern Belgian, German and Swiss equipment with only high-quality and ecologically clean raw materials.

To achieve our goals, we adhere to three fundamental principles of success:

Production development

Modern technology introduction

Enhancing a successful business model.

01

The company is actively working to promote SAG products abroad. To date, the high quality of the products offered has been appreciated by business partners from Russia, Kazakhstan, Azerbaijan, Armenia, Turkmenistan, Georgia, Afghanistan, Ukraine, China and the USA. The company successfully put into operation workshops for the production of tufted floor coverings (carpet) and artificial turf, production for the processing of raw cotton, workshops for the production of cotton yarn, polyester thread, and threads from recycled polyester (PET).

02

Carpets are woven on high-tech Belgian, German and Swiss equipment using only high-quality and environmentally friendly raw materials and materials, which ensures a high level of product quality. In order to ensure high world quality standards, the company has implemented the international quality management system ISO 9001:2008.

03

SAG is a team of like-minded professionals in which we respect and support each other, appreciate the contribution of everyone and listen to the opinions of others. Close team cooperation in an atmosphere of mutual respect and solidarity, customer focus and professionalism, responsibility for the future of the company and for the quality of our products. All these concepts form the basis of our values.
